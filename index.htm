Why I Chose HTML over Wix and Squarespace

So I was thinking about making a website but I wasn't sure what the best way to do it was. I had heard countles different companies trying to get me to sign up for their "Amazing Webiste Maker" ( wix and squarespace most notibly but there are also many others such as godaddy , clickfunnel , and weebly ) but did you ever stop to wonder what they used to make that website with? You know, the one that's trying to sell you on a monthly subscription and trying to convince you that you're not smart enough to learn to program.


Ok, maybe I'm being a little hard on them, after all they are trying to get more people into web design which is a wonderful thing, but I feel like trying to convince them that learning to code is just too hard isn't the way to do that. If you want to learn coding Khan Acedemy or w3 schools are great places to start learning for free, and by learning to program yourself you get all the advantages of making a website yourself with the flexibility of 100% custom html code.
